Bounty of Nature – Our Cocoa and Coffee

Sustainability – Our Promise, Our Future

Every sale gets reinvested into the farm, helping us sustain our practices and ensure that Konnela Farm continues to flourish. We take pride in producing 5 to 10 tons of premium cocoa and coffee beans each year, validated by top authorities like Sebehill Cocoa Produce Cooperative and LACRA.
